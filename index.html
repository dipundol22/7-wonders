A Journey Through Time

In 2007, more than 100 million people voted to declare the New Seven Wonders of the World. These magnificent structures represent the pinnacle of human creativity, engineering, and cultural heritage. Each wonder tells a unique story of ancient civilizations and their remarkable achievements.

The Seven Wonders

01

Great Wall of China

Spanning over 13,000 miles, this ancient defensive structure is one of the most impressive architectural feats in history. Built over centuries, it protected Chinese states from invasions.

About the New Seven Wonders

The New Seven Wonders of the World were chosen through a global poll conducted by the New7Wonders Foundation between 2000 and 2007. Over 100 million votes were cast by people around the world to select these remarkable monuments.

These structures were selected from a list of 200 existing monuments, representing the collective choice of millions who recognize their cultural significance, architectural innovation, and historical importance.
